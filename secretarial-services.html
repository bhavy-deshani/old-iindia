Secretarial Services

Our Secretarial Services are designed to support companies in maintaining compliance with regulatory requirements and managing corporate governance efficiently. We provide a comprehensive suite of services tailored to meet the needs of both new and existing companies.

Key Services Offered

  • Support for Incorporation of a New Company
  • Online Payment facility
  • Ongoing filing services for existing Companies: Annual Filing,Change in Director,creation of Charges,Rights Issue / Preferential Issue,Alteration of Memorandum etc.
  • Preparation / Maintenance of Minutes Book for Board Meeting,General Meetings (AGM & EGM)
  • Maintenance of all the forms filed MCA and related documents in the digital format. The companies can access all the secretarial documents with a secured Login.

MAINTENANCE OF STATUTORY REGISTERS

The following registers are to be maintained mandatorily by all Companies as per the provisions of Companies Act, 2013:

Sl. No Name of Register Penal Provisions
1 Register of Members If a Company defaults to maintain and update the Register of Members periodically, the company and every officer of the company who is in default shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.3,00,000
2 Register of Directors and Key Managerial Personnel The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
3 Register of Charges The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
4 Register of Share Application and Allotment The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
5 Register of Transfers The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
6 Register of Directors’ Shareholding The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
7 Register of investments The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
8 Register of Loans The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
9 Register of Fixed Assets The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
10 Register of Dividend The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
11 Register of Contracts and arrangements in which Directors are interested In case of failure to maintain the register of contracts and arrangements in which Directors are interested, every Director shall be liable to pay a penalty of Rs.25,000.
12 Seal Book The company and every officer of the company who is in default or such other person shall be punishable with fine which may extend to Rs.10,000.
