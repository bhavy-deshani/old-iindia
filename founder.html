Mr. P Vaidyanathan

Founder

P Vaidyanathan is a Chartered Accountant, Cost Accountant, and Company Secretary with over 5 decades of experience in India’s BFSI sector. In the 1970s, he moved away from his multi generational family’s chartered accountancy practice and began publishing financial articles in Tamil under the title ‘Vari Vikatan’. These writings simplified complex tax and investment concepts to promote financial education

He founded Integrated Enterprises (India) Pvt. Ltd in 1974.Integrated is an institution which pioneered financial Services for retail investors.

He has been part of many marquee corporate boards over decades. Some of his board memberships include

  • Non-Executive Chairman, City Union Bank Ltd. (CUB) (2009-2011)
  • Board Member, City Union Bank (1984–1992 & 2003–2011)
  • Director Franklin templeton Asset Management India pvt ltd till 2009.
  • Independent director Hatsun agro Products limited for 25 years.
  • Shareholder director VIjaya bank 2011 -2017
  • Member of Executive Committees, NSDL (8 years)
  • Independent Director, Edelweiss Tokio Life Insurance
  • Independent Director, Edelweiss Housing Finance Ltd.

With his invaluable corporate experience and insights, he continues to guide Integrated along with his passion for financial literacy and education.
