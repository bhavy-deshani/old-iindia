What is GIFT City / GIFT IFSC

  • GIFT City is India’s first operational Smart-City + International Financial Services Centre (IFSC), located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. It combines global-class infrastructure, a regulatory-friendly SEZ environment and services across Banking, Capital Markets, Fund Management, Insurance, FinTech, and more. The IFSC within GIFT City enables international and cross-border financial services by allowing transactions in foreign currencies and catering to clients outside the domestic Indian economy.
  • The regulatory oversight is by International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA), a unified regulator that subsumes the roles of domestic regulators like RBI, SEBI, IRDAI and PFRDA — ensuring a streamlined, global-standard regulatory regime.
  • GIFT IFSC offers attractive structural and fiscal benefits: companies/entities operating from there enjoy a special economic-zone (SEZ) status, with tax incentives, efficient regulatory and compliance framework, and access to global capital flows.
