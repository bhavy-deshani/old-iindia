What is INX GA — Global Access Platform

Single Integrated Account

India INX Global Access IFSC Ltd (INX GA) is an initiative that enables investors (Resident Indians, NRIs, and foreign investors) to access global markets — including US equities & ETFs, global stocks & ETFs, fixed-income instruments — through a single integrated account.

Accessible Investing

The user-friendly aspects: minimal account opening threshold (e.g. small amounts starting from USD 100), zero account opening fee, zero entry/exit or fixed custody charges in many cases (though management fees may apply) — making global investing more accessible.

In short — INX GA offers a bridge/doorway: a regulated, IFSC-based platform that allows Indian and global clients to tap into global securities, ETFs, bonds, and other instruments — with convenience, compliance, and flexibility.

Importantly:

  • INX GA acts only as a referrer/introducer to licensed international brokerage platforms (the “Introduced Broker(s)”).

  • That means clients must complete KYC, fund transfer, trade execution, settlement, and custody directly with the introduced broker — INX GA does not hold funds or shares, nor does it execute or settle trades.

  • INX GA does not provide investment advice, research reports, or recommendations.

  • All investment decisions, orders, and risk decisions rest with the client and the respective chosen broker.
